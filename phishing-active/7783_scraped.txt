Waterloo Region Market Update – Spring 2025 🌷Thinking about buying or selling this spring? Here’s what you need to know about the Waterloo Region real estate market!February Market Recap 📉February saw a slowdown in home sales due to economic uncertainty, tariff discussions, and winter weather. However, with the Bank of Canada’s recent rate cut on March 12, lowering the lending rate to 2.75%, we expect buyer activity to increase as borrowing costs become more affordable.With rates trending downward and further reductions anticipated, more buyers will likely enter the market in the coming months.Market Trends & Insights▪️ New listings are rising▪️ Buyer interest remains steady▪️ Mortgage pre-approvals are increasingThese are all signs that the market is shifting!Home Prices & Sales Breakdown▪️ Detached Homes – $900,003 avg. price (+1.0% YoY)▪️ Townhouses – $613,924 avg. price (-2.7% YoY)▪️ Semi-Detached – $670,352 avg. price (-0.5% YoY)▪️ Condos – $437,000 avg. price (-5.8% YoY)The overall average home price in Waterloo Region was $767,800, up 1.3% from last year and 1.8% from January.Inventory & Market Supply▪️ 858 new listings (+7.3% YoY)▪️ 1,450 active listings (+55.7% YoY)▪️ Homes are selling faster – 24 days on average (down from 34 days in January)💡 What does this mean for you?The combination of lower interest rates, rising inventory, and strong buyer demand makes this an exciting time to buy or sell!Thinking of selling this spring?
