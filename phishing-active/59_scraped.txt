What are some popular variants of the Matka Game?



There are many popular variants in the Satta Matka game, and they all offer unique gameplay to suit the individual needs of players. Here are some of the popular variants to know:


Single: This variant means players need to place a bet on numbers that range from 0 to 9. If the chosen number matches the results, then they win.


Jodi: It generally involves placing bets on a pair of numbers that range from 00 to 99. It is also known as the pair or Matka Jodi, and its gameplay is the same as Single.


Patti: In this variant, players need to place bets on different combinations of numbers that include Matka Open and Matka Close results. It especially revolves around three-digit numbers derived from the results.


Half Sangam: It includes both Single and Jodi variants. Under this, players need to choose a pair or a single number to win the match.


Full Sangam: This variant is the expanded version of Half Sangam that enables players to bet on multiple pairs with a single number.


Cycle Patti: Cycle Patti involves betting on a sequence of numbers. It comes from the result of previous rounds, and players need to assume the right sequence to place their bets effectively.
